## Regulation Protocol

### Early Warning Signs

- Feeling overwhelmed or behind
- Schedule slipping
- Playing from behind
- Emotional load accumulating
- Stability triggering restlessness (the itch — high sensation seeking)

### Three-Level Protocol

**Level 1 — Early slide:**

- 15-minute planning session — brain dump, reprioritize next 48 hours
- Reclaim the map

**Level 2 — Sliding further:**

- Workout — any form, any length, goal is completion
- Followed by reset planning session — 3 most important things, what can be deferred

**Level 3 — Full dysregulation:**

- Single point of contact — therapist or trusted person
- Drop to Minimum Viable System — no shame
- No new decisions while dysregulated (relationships, work, substances)
- Wait 24-48 hours before consequential decisions

### Minimum Viable System (bad weeks only)

- Sleep floor — non-negotiable
- No unplanned stimulant use — Ulysses contract holds
- Addie anchor — consistent daily touchpoint

### Ulysses Contracts (pre-decided, not negotiable in the moment)

- No unplanned stimulant use — cocaine or Adderall outside prescribed intentional use
- Stimulant use test: was this decided before the craving hit, from a regulated state?
- Social definition: weekend with intention, planned in advance
- No decisions while at Level 3
- Vaping stress protocol: text Lauren or Emma first before reaching for vape
